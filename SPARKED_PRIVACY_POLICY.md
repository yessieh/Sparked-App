Our design principle: collect as little as possible. This policy describes
what we actually collect and why.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account information** (only if you create an account): name, email,
city, optional bio and profile photo. Host workspaces additionally include
business/organizer name, logo, website, and social links you choose to make
public.

**Location.** Your device location or a ZIP code you enter is used **live**
to rank nearby events by distance. **We do not store a history of your
location or a log of events you've attended.** Location access is controlled
by your device permission and the in-app Location toggle.

**Listings and content you post:** event details, photos, descriptions —
public by design.

**Usage analytics (opt-in, default OFF):** anonymous usage statistics to
improve the product and, in aggregate, help organizers understand what
their community engages with. We do not sell this data.

**Payments.** Publishing fees are processed by Stripe. We do not receive or
store your full card number. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by
Stripe's privacy policy.

**Communications:** feedback you submit, reports you file, and emails you
send us.

## 2. What We Do NOT Do

- We do not sell your personal information.
- We do not use advertising trackers or sell ad targeting based on your
  data.
- We do not store location history.
- We do not rank the feed with a behavioral algorithm; events are ordered
  by distance.

## 3. How We Use Information

- Operate the platform (show nearby events, deliver notifications you've
  enabled, process listing payments and refunds);
- Send transactional messages (receipts, cancellation notices, weekly
  digest if you enable it);
- Enforce our Terms (moderation, report review, posting limits);
- Improve the product (opt-in analytics only);
- Comply with law.

collect personal information from them. Users must be 13+ (18+ to host).
If we learn we've collected data from a child under 13, we delete it.
